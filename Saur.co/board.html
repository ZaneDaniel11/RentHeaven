              Changelog

              By Castillo, June 1, 2023

              Saturday 22nd June 2024

              • Arena:
                - Added '/arena <msg>' to chat between players in the match, if it's a team gamemode, you can only chat with teammates.
                - Added 'enable altitude limit' setting to the Dogfight gamemode.
                - Added nametag colors (red for enemy, green for friendly).

              Thursday 30th May 2024

              • Weapons factory will now always deposit the crafted weapon in the inventory.

              Wednesday 29th May 2024

              • House system:
                - Changed the furniture UI
                - Furniture UI now opens with the 'F9' key (can still click the button).
                - Improved furniture spectate function, you can now move the camera around the object and zoom in/out.
                - Added favorites system for furniture.
                - Added more furniture objects.

              Wednesday 8th May 2024

              • Owned spawner auto-mechanic will now pay the nearest Pay n' Spray business owner.
              • Removed the 'produced X amount' business info message.

              Tuesday 7th May 2024

              • Added info text explaining how to hack an ATM.
              • Hot City will now change every 6 hours instead of every day at 00:00, the time to change is displayed at F2>Times.
              • Houses: you can no longer place furniture near the exit door.
              • Bank robbery: added a 5 seconds cooldown between interior usage.

              Friday 3rd May 2024

              • Deadline event: line color will now be red for opponents and green for your own line.
              • Arena:
                - Deadline: dying to your own line/speed limit/water will now give the kill to the nearest alive player.
                - Race: DD/Shooter: dying in the water will give the kill to the nearest alive player.
                - Dogfight: dying to map bounds area will now give the kill to the nearest alive player.

              • Added new event: Deadline

              Saturday 9th March 2024

              • Turf system:
                - Removed the 'Gangster' job.
                - Added 'turf duty' system, players on 'Criminal' job can toggle their turf duty from F1>Job.
                         1) can't go on turf duty from within War Zones or Doomsday event.
                         2) can't go on turf duty when in War Zones or Doomsday event queues.
                         3) player will be removed from turf duty when joining the War Zones or Doomsday events.
                         4) can't go on turf duty if you recently took damage.
                         5) on duty status will be displayed in the 'online members' clan panel.
                - Added 10 more turfs to San Fierro, making the amount equal to Las Venturas.
                - Added a 25 turf limit per group, any turf above the cap won't count towards paycheck.
                - Increased base paycheck per turf from $5,000 to $10,000 (this is actually a 10% increase in overall money).
                - If less than 4 turfers are on duty in the whole server, the paycheck will be further reduced by a third.
                - Added a 'hot city' event, which makes one of the two cities pay 20% more for the entire day, city rotates every day at 00:00 (server time).
                 
              • Added 'Gang of the Week', similar to 'Squad of the Week' in how it works.
                - Every Monday at 01:10 AM (server time) the gang which got the most points during the previous week will be picked as the winner.
                - At Sunday 00:00 (server time), said gang will lose the perks that were granted the previous week.
                - Points are granted for the following actions: capturing and defending turfs, kills within turfs, each action gives a different amount of points.
                        - Defending a turf has a 5 minutes cooldown to avoid abuse, and points are only granted if the turf was in the capture phase.
                - You can view the current Gang of the Week from the newly added button in the UCP clan tab, as well as the current points of every gang.
                - Winner will get 10% extra payment from all criminal activities.
                - Unlike Squad of the Week, the second and third positions will get money as a reward, with the winner getting the most amount.
                - The same gang can win as many weeks in a row as they can, there's no limitation.
                - We will expand on the rewards/perks in the future, suggestions are welcome.
                 
              • Added a tax system for groups, founders/HQs with the correct permission will be able to set a tax between 0 and 25%, the taxed money will be sent to the clan bank.
                - This system is only applied to turf paychecks at the moment.
                 
              • If you get kicked from the server while in War Zones or Doomsday, you will now be able to rejoin without a cooldown.

              Temporarily disabled systems

              By Castillo, May 26

              Due to the sudden emergence of a cheat we thought we were protected against, a player was able to trigger an unprotected script which allowed him to essentially generate money, this was possible due to the script being one of the oldest in the server (slot machines), which was poorly coded a decade ago, the script in cuestion is now removed until it can be properly rewritten.

              While most of our scripts are protected against such attacks, some older ones like the aforementioned one can slip through the cracks, meaning I will have to do a full revision of all potentially exploitable scripts.
              In order to achieve this while keeping the server open, I decided to take an extreme action, which is to disable all sort of trading in the server.

              The systems which are currently disabled until further notice are the following:

              • Money trading (/give or F2)
              • Trade system (entirely)
              • Clan bank and inventory (only deposit is disabled)
              • Clan payday
              • Used vehicle shop (both buy and sell)
              • Drug planting
              • Property purchashing (all of them)
              • Donator middleman
              • Profit for Ammunation and Food Shop businesses
              • Bribe
              • Drug and Arms dealer (/selldrugs and /sell)
              • Event hosting
              • Event donation bank
              • Arena money system (entry fee is automatically set to $0)
              • Vehicle inventory (deposit only)

              All the money which was generated by the cheater has been removed from the accounts, we will not take any sort complaints regarding incorrect amounts being removed.
